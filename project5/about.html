About This Site

These recipes were chosen for affordability, minimal equipment, and flexibility. Each page lists ingredient swaps and dietary notes to help you adapt.

Dietary Notes

  • Gluten-free: Choose certified GF grains and sauces.
  • Dairy-free: Use plant-based yogurt, cheese, or skip dairy toppings.
  • Vegetarian: Swap chicken or fish with beans, tofu, or extra veggies.

Basic Equipment

  • Knife and cutting board
  • Microwave or stovetop (and a pan)
  • Baking sheet or oven-safe dish (for salmon)
  • Measuring cups/spoons (or approximate with common household items)
