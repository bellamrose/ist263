Chicken Burrito Bowl

Serves: 4 · Time: 35–40 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cooked rice (white or brown)
  • 1 lb boneless chicken (breast or thigh), diced
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • 1 tsp chili powder
  • 1/2 tsp cumin
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 can (15 oz) black beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 cup corn (frozen or canned, drained)
  • 1 cup salsa
  • Optional: shredded cheese, diced avocado, chopped cilantro, lime wedges, Greek yogurt or sour cream

Instructions

  1. Cook rice according to package directions (or use leftover rice).
  2. Season chicken with chili powder, cumin, salt, and pepper.
  3. Heat oil in a pan over medium heat. Cook chicken 6–8 minutes until browned and cooked through.
  4. Warm beans and corn in a small pot or microwave.
  5. Assemble bowls: rice, chicken, beans, corn, and salsa. Add optional toppings.
  6. Squeeze lime over the top and serve.

Modifications

  • Vegetarian: Swap chicken for extra beans, tofu, or roasted veggies.
  • Dairy-free: Skip cheese and use dairy-free yogurt if desired.
  • Gluten-free: Use GF salsa and seasonings.
  • Microwave-only: Use rotisserie chicken or canned chicken; assemble and heat bowls in the microwave.
