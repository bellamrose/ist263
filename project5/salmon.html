Lemon Garlic Salmon

Serves: 2 · Time: 20–25 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2 salmon fillets (about 4–6 oz each)
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 clove garlic, minced (or 1/2 tsp garlic powder)
  • 1 lemon (zest and juice)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Optional: dried parsley or dill

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 400°F (205°C). Line a baking sheet with foil.
  2. Pat salmon dry. Place on the sheet, skin-side down.
  3. Mix oil, garlic, lemon zest, and half the lemon juice. Brush over salmon. Season with salt, pepper, and herbs.
  4. Bake 10–14 minutes, depending on thickness, until flakes easily with a fork.
  5. Squeeze remaining lemon juice over the top and serve.

Modifications

  • No oven: Pan-sear 3–4 minutes per side, or air-fry 8–10 minutes at 390°F.
  • Dairy-free: This recipe is naturally dairy-free.
  • Gluten-free: Naturally GF; verify spice blends.
  • Budget tip: Use frozen fillets; thaw in the fridge and pat dry before cooking.
