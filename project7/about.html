About This Site

These recipes were chosen for affordability, minimal equipment, and flexibility. Each page lists ingredient swaps and dietary notes to help you adapt the meals to your needs and preferences.

Dietary Notes

  • Gluten-free: Choose certified gluten-free grains, tortillas, and sauces.
  • Dairy-free: Use plant-based yogurt, cheese, or skip dairy toppings entirely.
  • Vegetarian: Swap chicken or fish with beans, tofu, or extra vegetables.

Basic Equipment

  • Knife and cutting board
  • Microwave or stovetop with a pan
  • Baking sheet or oven-safe dish (for sheet-pan meals)
  • Measuring cups and spoons, or approximate with common household items
